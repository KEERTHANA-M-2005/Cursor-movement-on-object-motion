Welcome to Virtual Mouse!

This project transforms your webcam into a smart, hands-free virtual mouse. You can move the cursor, click, control volume, and brightness — all using simple hand gestures!

Built with powerful technologies like Python, OpenCV, and Mediapipe.

Features

  • Cursor Control: Move your mouse cursor smoothly using hand gestures.
  • Click & Scroll: Click and scroll with simple hand movements, making it easy to navigate.
  • Volume & Brightness Control: Adjust your system's volume and brightness using intuitive gestures.
